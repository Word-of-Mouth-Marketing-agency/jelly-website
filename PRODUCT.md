## Users

Egyptian consumers, broad market, all ages. Shopping on mobile and desktop. Browsing for fun, colorful socks with good value — often as gifts or everyday wear. Bilingual audience (Arabic-primary and English-comfortable). Price-aware but willing to pay for personality and quality.

## Product Purpose

Jelly is an Egyptian socks brand whose tagline is "Socks that make you smile." The website is the primary retail storefront: homepage, category pages, product detail, cart, checkout, and account. Success means a customer who arrives knowing nothing about Jelly leaves with a pair in their cart and a smile on their face.

## Brand Personality

Playful, warm, Egyptian. Voice: conversational, cheerful, unhurried. Never pushy. Bilingual with equal care for Arabic and English.

## Anti-references

- **Generic Shopify store:** default layout, zero personality, every product displayed the same way. If it could sell any product from any country, it has failed.
- **Corporate minimalism:** Apple/Muji-style whitespace and cold precision. Too serious for a brand about joy and socks.
- **Loud pattern overload:** every surface covered in textures and patterns. Products drown in decoration.

## Design Principles

1. **Joy first.** Every interaction — a hover, a badge, a button — should feel like a small pleasure, not a transaction. If it is neutral, it is not trying hard enough.
2. **Egyptian warmth.** Local identity shows in rhythm, color, and tone. This is not a generic international DTC brand. It lives here.
3. **Products are the stars.** Socks are inherently visual and expressive. Layout, color, and composition exist to make the product the focal point, never to replace it.
4. **Breathe before you fill.** Bold color is used deliberately — section by section, not all at once. Whitespace is not emptiness; it is the pause before the next smile.
5. **Everyone's welcome.** Broad market, broad age range, two languages. WCAG AA, legible type, clear CTAs. Accessibility is part of being warm, not a polish step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Bilingual (Arabic RTL, English LTR) with equal design care. Focus states visible. Contrast ratios met for all text. Reduced motion accommodated via `prefers-reduced-motion` where animations are present.
